Design, development and hands on making assets out of clay.

Although it wasnt a commercial succes it pionered many unique features for 2013 including:

- Seamless transition from startup screen to real app.
- 60fps performance.
- Zero [loading screens](https://vimeo.com/60732836).
- Custom [oriantation change](https://vimeo.com/60392214) animation.
