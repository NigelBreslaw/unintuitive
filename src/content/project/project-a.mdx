## Ishtar Commander for Destiny 2 (2015-Present)

Sole design and development project. Written entirely in a cross platfrom prototyping framework called [ZingStudio.io](https://www.zingstudio.io/).

iPhone, iPad and Android.

2 million downloads (equal split iOS / Android).

4.6 star rating on the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ishtar-commander-for-destiny-2/id1030835428) and 4.4 stars on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.unintuitive.IshtarCommander&hl=en&gl=US).
